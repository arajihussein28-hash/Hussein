Product & Target Audience

This project showcases a detailed Monkey D. Luffy collectible figure with multiple hand accessories and a solid base. It is designed for anime lovers and collectors who enjoy owning and displaying characters from the famous One Piece series.

Specific Features

FeatureDescription
DetailingBeautifully hand-painted finish with accurate color and shading.
AccessoriesIncludes interchangeable hands and gloves for unique poses.
Display BaseStable circular base ideal for display on desks and shelves.
PackagingComes in a collector’s box with printed artwork and window display.
